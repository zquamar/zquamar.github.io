What is CGP?

CGP is referred to as a cumulative grade point. CGP gives the average grade point of the student performance in all the subjects excluding the additional Subject. The subject wise performance of the students is recorded in forms of Grades. The CGP is used to denote the overall performance of the student in the academic session as grades. The CGP system was introduced to ease the burden of marks and the stress of the examinations among students. Due to this reason, the CGP system is also used in the bachelor's and Master's Degree.

Steps to calculate your CGP

Step 1 : Navigate to your respective department or click on navbar icon beside home button.
Step 2 : Select your semester.
Step 3 : Enter your grades in respective semester. Please make sure to input every option!!
Step 4 : Click on calculate button to reflect your CGP.
Step 5 : Click on reset to refresh the page.
